How to Map Buying Stakeholders

This workshop provides a structured process for identifying the key players in a complex B2B sales motion. You will learn to move beyond job titles to understand the motivations, challenges, and buying personas of each stakeholder, and build an actionable plan to drive consensus and close deals.

Note: Your progress is automatically saved in this browser's local storage. This data is private to your computer and is not sent to any server.

  • 1 Map the Buying Jobs
    Identify the 2-3 most critical and difficult 'jobs' your customer must complete to make a purchase.
  • 2 Assign Personas to Roles
    Build a detailed map of the buying group, defining each stakeholder's functional role, buying persona, and key motivations.
  • 3 Develop a Buyer Enablement Plan
    Translate your map into action. Create 'prescriptions' to help your champion overcome objections and build consensus.
